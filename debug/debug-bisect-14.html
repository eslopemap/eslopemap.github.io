Bug reproducer: terrain-analysis invisible in 3D when many layers intervene

Library: @eslopemap/maplibre-gl@5.21.2 (fork with built-in terrain-analysis layer type)
Bug: When 20+ non-DEM layers (GeoJSON line/circle) are added between the hillshade layer and the terrain-analysis layer in the style stack, the terrain-analysis overlay becomes invisible in 3D terrain mode.
Expected: Layer stack position should not affect terrain-analysis rendering in 3D.
Workaround: Place terrain-analysis layers immediately after the hillshade in the initial style.
